The Challenge They Faced

But for all its technical strength, the platform had a visibility problem. Great tools don't scale themselves, and Dimers was sitting on untapped growth because the market simply didn't know they existed. They needed a partner who could translate complex betting advantages into scroll-stopping creative and turn quiet quality into measurable acquisition. That's where Valiant came in.

Dimers had built a legitimately strong product. Proprietary algorithms, real-time edge calculations, tools that gave bettors an actual advantage over the sportsbooks.

Pricing was competitive, features kept shipping, and the user experience was clean. But none of that mattered if nobody knew about it.

The platform was trapped in the classic builder's dilemma: great product, zero awareness. Organic growth was slow, paid acquisition had never been systematically executed, and their creative library was nearly non-existent.

To break through in a crowded, skeptical market full of pick-sellers and tout services, Dimers needed a creative engine and a paid strategy that could introduce the brand at scale while positioning it as a serious data-driven alternative to guesswork betting.

What We've Done

When we partnered with Dimers, the brief was clear: take a good product with previously humble marketing, and make it impossible to ignore. That meant building a creative system from scratch, standing up paid acquisition at scale, and positioning the brand in a way that separated it from the noise of competitors flooding the sports betting space.

We started by identifying target personas and building Dimers a real creative engine. The platform's value proposition was technical, proprietary data, edge calculations, tools that identify value bets, but technical doesn't sell to cold audiences. We translated those advantages into fast, digestible video ads designed for bettors scrolling through their feeds. Each script opened with a hook that hit a pain point: losing streaks, bad beats, chasing losses, betting blind. Then we pivoted into proof: real tools, real edges, real outcomes. The tone stayed credible, never hyped, never promising guarantees, just showing bettors there was a smarter way to play. Within months, we delivered a steady pipeline of video and static ads, giving Dimers the creative firepower to test, iterate, and scale without ever running dry.

With creative locked in, we launched Meta acquisition campaigns designed to introduce Dimers to cold audiences and convert warm traffic. Cold campaigns targeted sports bettors across football, basketball, and betting-adjacent interest graphs, leading with education-first hooks and demonstrations of how the tools actually worked. Retargeting campaigns chased site visitors, free trial sign-ups, and content engagers, pushing them toward conversion with social proof, feature highlights, and limited-time incentives. The funnel was clean: attract attention with value, retarget with urgency, convert with clarity. Over the course of the partnership, these campaigns delivered consistent new user growth, scaling Dimers' paid acquisition from zero to a repeatable, profitable channel.

The Black Friday campaign became a standout moment in the calendar. We built a dedicated creative kit around the promotion, leaning into urgency, seasonal timing, and the psychology of year-end betting. Video ads highlighted Dimers' edge-finding capabilities with direct CTAs tied to the limited offer. Static ads reinforced scarcity and value across placements, and made clear the value customers would get out of the next three months. The result was one of Dimers' strongest conversion periods, proving the brand could capitalize on tentpole moments when the creative and strategy were dialed in.

Beyond paid ads, we invested in educational content designed to onboard and retain users. Dimers' toolset was powerful, but only if people knew how to use it. We produced a library of tutorial videos breaking down core features: how to read edge percentages, how to identify value bets, how to navigate “best props” and live betting tools. These tutorials were embedded into email journeys for new sign-ups and hosted on-site as evergreen resources. The goal wasn't just to acquire users, it was to activate them, turning trial sign-ups into engaged, retained bettors who understood the platform's full value.

What started as a visibility problem became a growth engine. Dimers now has a scalable creative pipeline, a proven paid acquisition system, and educational content that turns curious sign-ups into power users. The product was always strong. Now the market knows it.
