our story

Forged in Fire. Ready for Battle.

In 2019, we founded Juiced.io, a software company in the make money online niche, and generated over two million in revenue. However, scaling was extremely difficult. Our biggest bottleneck? Talent.

After months of trying out freelancers and other marketers, nobody understood our niche well enough to get us results. We decided to take things into our own hands.

Fast-forward to today and we’ve built a team of the best marketing talent in the iGaming & Finance space: professional video editors, veteran advertising experts, dedicated email marketers, graphic designers, you name it.

Valiant is the elite team we always wanted to have when we first started Juiced. Businesses that work with us get results because we understand; We only succeed when you do.

our values

Pursuit of excellence

Good enough is the enemy of greatness. We obsess over every detail because the smallest difference separates winners from losers. Whether it's a single word in a script or a pixel in a design, perfection isn't optional — it's the standard that built $200M+ in client revenue.

Lightning-speed execution

While others schedule meetings about meetings, we've already launched three campaigns. 9-day onboarding vs. industry standard 6-8 weeks. Same-day responses within 4 hours. Speed isn't just an advantage — it's our weapon against slow, bureaucratic agencies.

obsession with results

We don't chase vanity metrics or participation trophies. 2x ROAS minimum. 40%+ email open rates. 2x view growth by month one. Every campaign is measured, optimized, and accountable because clients pay for results, not effort.

unbreakable mentality

We win together or not at all. No egos, no politics, no corporate bullshit. From Miami yacht adventures to equity sharing, every crew member has skin in the game because revolutionizing marketing isn't a solo mission — it's a pirate raid.
