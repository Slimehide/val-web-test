our story

Forged in Fire. Ready for Battle.

In 2022, Valiant started as a marketing agency.

We worked with hundreds of companies, helped brands grow across some of the most competitive industries online, and learned what great marketing really takes.

Strategy matters. Creative matters. Speed matters. Reporting matters. But the biggest lesson was that marketing only works when all of those pieces move together, every week, without momentum being lost.

Running an agency gave us the playbook. It also showed us the limits of the model.

Even with strong people, strong systems, and strong results, agency work is still built around manual execution. Every campaign requires coordination. Every client has different goals, data, channels, approvals, and bottlenecks.

The knowledge compounds, but the delivery still depends on people having enough time to execute it consistently.

So we started building something different.

ValiantAI is the platform we wish existed when we were running the agency. It takes the strategy, creative process, campaign execution, reporting, and performance learnings we developed over years of client work, and turns them into one AI marketing system.

The result is everything businesses want from a great agency: expertise, execution, creative output, and growth strategy, delivered with the speed, consistency, and control of software.

our values

Pursuit of excellence

We believe great marketing is built in the details. Every word, visual, campaign, and optimization matters. Valiant was created to bring that level of care, consistency, and precision into every part of the marketing process.

Lightning-speed execution

Marketing slows down when every campaign depends on human bandwidth. ValiantAI was built to change that. By automating the full workflow from strategy and creative to publishing, optimization, analytics, and reporting, the platform keeps campaigns moving without the manual work that normally holds growth back.

obsession with results

Activity is not growth. ValiantAI was built to measure what matters, learn from every campaign, and keep improving performance across ads, email, and social. The goal is not more marketing work. The goal is better marketing outcomes.

unbreakable mentality

Great companies are built by teams who share the mission. At Valiant, we believe in ownership, trust, and moving as one. Every person contributes to the same goal: building an autonomous marketing platform that gives companies a better way to grow.

our mission

Our mission is to make world-class marketing execution accessible to every business, without the cost, delays, or limits of human-led agencies.
