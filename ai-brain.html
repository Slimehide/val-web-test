Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ValiantAI

What is Valiant?

Valiant is a fully autonomous marketing platform that replaces your entire marketing team. It researches your business, builds your strategy, then creates, publishes, and optimizes your video ads, graphics, emails, and social posts across every channel. A whole marketing department, running on its own.

Does it really run on its own?

Yes. From researching your brand to writing, designing, filming, publishing, and optimizing your campaigns, Valiant handles it all autonomously. You decide how hands-on to be: let it run completely on its own, or review and approve everything first. The work gets done either way.

Will my content look AI-generated?

No. Our AI avatars look and sound genuinely human, and every video, graphic, and email is built from your brand and shaped by real marketers, not generic AI output. Your content comes across as professionally made, the kind you'd expect from a top agency, not a machine.

Can I control what goes live?

Completely. You choose your level of control on every campaign: let Valiant publish automatically, approve the final version first, or sign off at each step. Pause, edit, or switch anything off whenever you want. It does the heavy lifting, but you always have the final say.

How much does Valiant cost?

There's a plan for every stage, from starter to enterprise. Each is a monthly subscription plus simple usage-based pricing for the content you create, so you only ever pay for what you produce. Visit our pricing page for the full breakdown.
